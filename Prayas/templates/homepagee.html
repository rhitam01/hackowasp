Services

Our service offers a comprehensive solution to monitor and manage electricity usage, including monitoring electricity income and usage. By tallying the electricity income and usage after each day, our service can detect any discrepancies that may indicate electricity theft or other unauthorized usage. This feature helps our customers to identify potential electricity theft, significantly impacting their energy bills and revenue.
‍
Additionally, our system provides real-time alerts to our users if they are using electricity excessively, if electricity is being wasted, or if it is being stolen. Our intelligent algorithms can provide strong suggestions on when and when not to use electricity, and our service can automatically turn off electricity in a particular room if there is no activity detected. These features not only help our users save on their energy bills but also contribute towards a more sustainable and greener environment.
‍
Moreover, our service can generate reports and insights into our customers 'energy consumption patterns, helping them optimize their energy usage and reduce their energy bills. By using our service, our customers can achieve better energy efficiency and sustainability while also reducing their operational costs and enhancing their bottom line.

In summary, our service offers a comprehensive solution to monitor and manage electricity usage, helping our customers identify potential electricity theft, reduce their energy bills, and achieve better energy efficiency and sustainability.
